01

Four-Bar Linkage — Container Challenge

Mechanism Synthesis Kinematics Linkage Design
+

Overview

Designed a four-bar linkage to automate a trash container tray. The tray starts flat on top (position A), moves out for user deposit, then rotates to dump contents into a rear opening (position B) — without the linkage crossing the container interior. Pivots constrained to container side walls only.

Requirements

  • Tray moves from top (A) to side dump position (B)
  • Pivot points on container sides only, no extensions
  • Linkage must not traverse the container interior
  • Positions A and B are the extremes of motion

Key Dimensions

Top opening35 cm (15 + 5 + 15)
Vertical drop A → B15 cm
Side opening height5 cm from base
Pivot constraintSide walls only

Approach

  • Graphical two-position synthesis through perpendicular bisectors
  • Verification of motion extremes at A and B
